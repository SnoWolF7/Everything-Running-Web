Name

Exercises

Hips/Glutes

Posterior Chain

Fire hydrants, donkey kicks, hip circles, hold last rep of each set for 10 sec

Bridge Core

90 sec each: walk outs, frog bridges, one legged bridges.

Baranov Squat Core

20 squats, 19, 18, …, 10 (30 sec rest), …, 5 (15 sec rest)

Hell Week 2018 pt. 1

90 sec each: walking bridge combo, single leg swivel, grasshopper, hamstring curls

Hell Week 2018 pt. 2

90 sec each: hamstring curls, triangle butts, circle butts, I butts

Upper Leg

Squat Core

25 squats, 90 sec wall sits

Descending Side Lunges and Squats

Alternate between side lunges and squats: 20 side lunges, 19 squats, …, 2 side lunges, 1 squat. 15 sec rest between.

Hip Flexor Core

Hold the knee at <90º, @90º, >90º, and down. 30 sec each position

Shane Lunge Core

60 sec alternating sprinter lunges, 60 sec regular lunges, 45 sec split jumps

Wall Sit Core

Wall Sits at intervals of 1 min, 1.5, 2, 2.5, 3, 2, 1. Equal rest. Or use a random number generator.

Kill Your Quads Core

Alternating one legged squats, 1 ½ squats, jump squats

Quadsilva Core

30 squats, 30 reverse lunges, 90 sec wall sits

Great Wall of Pain

30 sec each: wall sits, single leg, switch, open/closed, heels off

Lower Leg

Jump Rope Core

60 sec slow, 30 rapid, repeat 3 times

Mixed

Classic Leg Core

45 sec split jumps, 30 sec alternating toe taps, 30 sec simultaneous toe taps, 45 sec jump squats, 45 sec calf raisers, 60 sec wall sit.

Quad & Hip Core

45-60 sec each: split jumps, calf raises, jump squats, wall sits

Resistance Core

Clams, elevated open bridges, crab walks, wall sits (with resistance band and increasing duration, 30 → 45 → 60)

Hip Clock Core

Extend leg forward, 45º diagonally, lateral abduction, 45º diagonally, backward. Hold the last rep of each direction. Focus on contraction while in the air. Mirror on the other side.

Senan Core

40 squats, 30 calf raisers, 20 lunges, 10 jump squats

Alt: Seconds instead of reps, +10
