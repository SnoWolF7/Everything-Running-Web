Group

Circle of Death

Form a line/circle. Hold the push-up position as everyone does 1/2/3/2/1 push-ups one at a time (or is it?)

Marine

Find three other people of similar weight & height. Pile one on top of each other to form a square. Push up and hold at the same time.

Two-Man Hold

Find a partner of similar height and weight. Crissc-ross legs and push up. Similar to Marine.

Poker Cards

Crabbe will distribute a randomly shuffled card to each group. Complete the number of pushups listed.
J, Q, K = {10}
Diamonds = diamonds, clove = wide-arms, heart = regular, spade = double
