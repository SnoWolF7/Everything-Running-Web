Rehab: Shins/Calves


Ankle Exercises

Many of the exercises used for the ankle are effective in building a strong foundation for the lower legs (shins and calves).

Hips-Glutes Exercises

Many of the exercises used for the pelvic region are effective in building a strong foundation for the lower legs (shins and calves).

Toe Walks

Walk on the balls of your feet on both sides. Take controlled steps. Repeat with walking on the big toe and outside of the front feet.

Heel Walks

Rotate ankle toward midline of body. There should not be any knee movement.

Lean Forward Balance

Calf Raiser

Stand flat on the ground. Lift heels such that only balls of feet are touching the ground. Up fast and down slow. Variations: leveled surface, single legged, towel, free weights.

Single Legged Hops

Stand on one foot. Hop on one foot in a square shape in barefoot. Variations are Square, Lateral, Upward, Downward.
