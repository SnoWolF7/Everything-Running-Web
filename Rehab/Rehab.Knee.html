Rehab: Knee


Single Legged Balance

Stand on one foot while the other hangs in the air. No specified angle. Start at intervals of 20 seconds and move up as you see fit. Rotate ankle in such a way to trace each letter of the alphabet. You can supplement by throwing a volleyball against a wall or a medicine ball in a circle group.

Single Legged Dips

Stand on one foot flat on the ground. Bend at the knee slightly but does not go toward either side. Bonus for not going beyond the toes. Repeat. Variation: hold it at that bent

Squats

Stand flat on the ground on both feet. Lower your body as if you are going to sit down. Keep knees pointing in the same direction as your feet. Maintain balance the entire time.

Lunges

Start standing up. Drive one knee up and let it drop to the ground. There should be two 90ยบ angles formed at your knees, one in the front and another in the back. Continue and change which leg is at front.

Split Jumps

Start at a lunge position. Hop upward and change the leg at the front in midair. Land and repeat. Swing your arms as if you're running.

Wall Sits

Find a solid wall. Lean against the wall. Lower yourself such that your knees form a 90ยบ angle with your thigh. Hold for the duration.

Streamlined Bulgarian Squats

Takes the base of a regular squat. Find a platform of appropriate height. Place hind leg on the platform and front leg one to two steps forward. Squat down or lower your body weight. Keep knee as stable as possible on the way down aka do not exceed the toes. Keep everything slow and steady.

Hop To Sit

Start standing on one leg. Slightly hop upward. Move backward a little and land sitting on a surface.

Knee Dips

Find a stable elevated surface or platform. Stand on it with one foot hanging off the edge. Mimic a single footed squat as the other leg drops toward the ground. This motion should be fast and controlled. Come back up slow and repeat.

Band Quad-Knee Extension

Find a squat or lift machine. Remove any obstacles. Place a resistance band (extra stretch kind) on the front legs of the machine. Place the bands over you at the knee pit while you are in a knees down pushup position. Straighten your legs as you push upward. Focus on contraction and hold for 1-2 seconds. Return to starting position and repeat.

Jump Squat to Lateral Cut

Start with a jump squat. Immediately cut to either left or right. For group activity, have one person point to either side and swap people around.

Speed Down to Cut

Start with running. Suddenly slow down and cut to either left or right. For group activity, have one person point to either side and swap people around.

1, 2, 3 Wooden Man

Run toward a target. Suddenly slow down and run backward. Repeat. For group activity, have one person signal change in direction.

Cariocas

Jog sideways. Hover one leg over the other, then under. Focus on the form.
